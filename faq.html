Common Questions you may have

What should I wear?

Our dress policy is formal. This means dress shirt and pants for those who wish to wear a suit. No tie is needed but we love to see them!

What is the weather like?

The ceremony and reception will both be held outdoors. This means it will be chilly so please bring a sweater or jacket San Diego is not always warm especially in October.

What is the parking situation?

There is a parking lot at the venue. We will have a shuttle to take you to and from the venue and hotel. Please do not drink and drive.
