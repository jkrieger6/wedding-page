Sarah and Jake's story

On our first date, we went to a local sunset spot with Tollie and enjoyed a picnic and the ocean views. We had a great time and immediately were inseperable from that day forward.We knew we had something special. After a few months of dating, we decided to move in together. We've been living together ever since.

Together we love to travel anywhere we can go. We have traveled around the states exploring as many places as we can see. Sarah and myself have taken trips to Mexico, Indonesia, Portugal and Spain and we have many more trips planned for the future.

In the winter of 2022, we got engaged. Now this October 2024, we are getting married. We're so excited to start this new chapter of our lives together and can't wait to celebrate with you!
